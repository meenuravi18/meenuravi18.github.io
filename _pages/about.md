I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Virginia Tech. My research lies at the intersection of geospatial data management and natural language processing. My focus is on developing methods that enhance natural language understanding and reasoning over geospatial data to support decision-making in domains such as natural hazard response, environmental monitoring, and urban planning.
